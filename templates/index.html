THE ENVIRONMENT

Through our network of suppliers and contractors, we offer clients options to reduce the environmental impact from deinstallation projects, while minimising costs through asset recovery. This can take the form of re-selling of your equipment, and/or recycling of waste materials like steel.

OUR VISION

In the coming decades, we see a strong need for specific expertise to manage the de-installation of end-of-life equipment and facilities. As the rate of technological change increases, the need for clients to replace new with old will also increase. In certain cases, the dismantling of old equipment and facilities increases the free space of a building to make it more attractive for future installations of newer technology and equipment. With the presence of hazardous materials, such as asbestos and lead based materials, or process contamination from previous processes, having the necessary expertise to manage such deinstallation works is crucial to maintaining safety and environmental control of deinstallation works.
