DISPOSAL

We provide specialist engineering consultancy and management services for the decommissioning, decontamination, deinstallation & disposal of end-of-life facilities and equipment.

MORE

ABOUT Our Disposal Service

FUNDAMENTALS

Our disposal service will always be defined in our environmental and waste management plan. Within this document, we define our waste streams and employ reputable companies who dispose of waste as governed by local environmental regulations. Hazardous waste material will be packed into waste receptacles by the waste disposal company to ensure environmental and health & safety compliance.

Full traceability will be provided on waste generated by your dismantling project. Documentation is maintained to track the waste from source (the project work zone) to the final destination of the waste.

RECYCLING

EOL Engineering will always aim to maximise what waste materials can be recycled. Through our network of waste management companies. we will ensure that you get the best return on waste that can be recycled and has value e.g mild steel and stainless steel. Our careful segregation of metals during dismantling will ensure we can maximise your rebate.

RE-SALE

In conjunction with our preferred equipment re-selling companies, we will make every effort to re-sell your equipment to reputable companies should you wish to try to re-coup some of the value that remains in your equipment.

If you are interested in the option of selling your equipment, it is worthwhile considering this during the front end of your project to maximise the time that the equipment can be advertised globally. EOL Engineering will ensure that equipment being resold is carefully extracted from your building and shipped to the purchasing company.
